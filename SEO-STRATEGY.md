## Strategic Positioning

BLACKFOX competes in a global, commoditised market where buyers default to the cheapest offshore vendor. The primary audience is Europe (UK, Germany, Netherlands, Scandinavia), followed by the USA. The competitive advantage is the combination of **hand-traced quality** (pen-tool, not auto-select), **80+ in-house designers** (no subcontracting), **3-step QC**, and a **free trial** with no commitment. SEO must communicate these differentiators at every touchpoint.

## E-E-A-T Strategy

### Experience
- Add "since 2016" timestamps and founding story to About page
- Publish before/after case studies with client industry context
- Show the 3-step QC process in detail (visual infographic)
- Display team count ("80+ designers") prominently on key pages

### Expertise
- Create author profiles for senior retouchers (Person + ProfilePage schema)
- Write technique-level blog content (frequency separation, pen-tool paths, etc.)
- Add credentials for technique certifications if applicable
- Detail workflow methodology on the About page

### Authoritativeness
- Build citations on Clutch, DesignRush, Sortlist, GoodFirms
- Earn links from photography blogs and e-commerce communities
- Submit to "best photo editing services" roundup articles
- Sponsor or guest-post on photography and e-commerce newsletters

### Trustworthiness
- Display international contact details (European/US point-of-contact or phone) alongside Bangladesh address to build trust with target markets
- Add client logos (with permission) or industry type badges
- Prominent "Free Trial — no payment required" CTA reduces risk
- SSL, privacy policy, T&C all already present — ensure schema reflects this
